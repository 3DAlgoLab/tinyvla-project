How to Replace a Bathroom Faucet

  1. Turn off the water supply to your sink.
  2. Place a towel and bucket below the sink’s drain pipe.
  3. Disconnect the P-trap and sink drain if you’re replacing the drain too.
  4. Remove the old faucet by unscrewing the water supply lines and lock nuts under the sink.
  5. Clean the sink surface to remove any old gasket material or debris.
  6. Install the new faucet by setting it into the mounting holes and securing it with lock nuts.
  7. Reconnect the water supply lines to the new faucet.
  8. Turn on the water supply and check for any leaks.
  9. Test the faucet to ensure it is working properly.
