Top 10 Things to Do in South Beach, Miami

  • 1. South Pointe Park and Pier

    Enjoy stunning views of Miami’s skyline and the Atlantic Ocean, perfect for a relaxing day out.

  • 2. Lummus Park

    Experience the vibrant atmosphere with beach volleyball, outdoor gyms, and beautiful sandy beaches.

  • 3. Ocean Drive

    Stroll along this iconic street lined with Art Deco buildings, restaurants, and nightlife.

  • 4. Lincoln Road Mall

    Shop, dine, and people-watch at this popular pedestrian street filled with stores and cafes.

  • 5. The Bass

    Explore contemporary art exhibits in this renowned museum located in a historic Art Deco building.

  • 6. Miami Beach Botanical Garden

    Relax in this lush, tropical garden featuring native plants, a Japanese garden, and a butterfly garden.

  • 7. Holocaust Memorial

    Visit this poignant memorial dedicated to the victims of the Holocaust, featuring a striking bronze sculpture.

  • 8. Española Way

    Discover this charming, historic street with Mediterranean-style buildings, shops, and restaurants.

  • 9. Art Deco Historic District

    Take a guided tour to learn about the unique architecture and history of South Beach’s Art Deco buildings.

  • 10. Nikki Beach

    Unwind at this famous beach club known for its lively atmosphere, great food, and beachfront location.
